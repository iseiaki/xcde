At XCentuate UX, we are on a mission to transform the way businesses connect with their current and future customers. We use inclusive research methods to help clients deeply understand their customers' needs, wants, and desires. We do this by going beneath the surface to understand a diversity of lived experiences and how these experiences impact behaviors, motivations, expectations, and pain points.

Our approach to research and design uncovers customer frustrations and identifies opportunities to address their unmet needs, leading to innovative, engaging products and services that resonate with wider audiences.

Ursula Wright, PhD

Principal Researcher

Dr. Ursula Wright is the Research Director and Principal Researcher at XCentuate UX Inc. She created XCentuate UX to help teams leverage technology to build inclusive products for both under-invested communities and the broader population. She has a passion for creating equitable experiences for people often excluded from the design process, including those with physical and cognitive disabilities. She has 20+ years of experience in human-centered research and design. Her work has influenced products made by Twilio, Intuit, Microsoft, IKEA, Guidewire, General Motors, government, and academic institutions.

A message from Dr. Wright

" Inclusive design is at the core of everything we do. We firmly believe that a product or service should be accessible to everyone, regardless of physical ability, age, income, or cultural background. Our approach to design includes helping teams connect with people of diverse lived experiences and designing innovative products that resonate with a broader audience. "
